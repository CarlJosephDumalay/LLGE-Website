About Us

LLGE — Local Leather Good Exposura

LLGE is a proudly Filipino brand dedicated to showcasing the craftsmanship and artistry of locally made leather goods. Established with a passion for quality and authenticity, LLGE creates premium leather bags that blend timeless design with modern Filipino style.

Each piece is carefully handcrafted by skilled local artisans using sustainably sourced materials, ensuring durability, elegance, and character in every stitch. From everyday carry essentials to statement fashion pieces, LLGE represents more than just leather — it’s a movement to elevate local talent and celebrate the rich heritage of Filipino craftsmanship.

At LLGE, we believe in “carrying culture with class.”
