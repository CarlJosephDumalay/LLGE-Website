About Us

LLGE — Local Leather Good Exposura

LLGE is a proudly Filipino brand dedicated to showcasing the craftsmanship and artistry of locally made leather goods.

Each piece is handcrafted by skilled artisans using sustainably sourced materials, ensuring durability and elegance in every stitch.

At LLGE, we believe in “carrying culture with class.”
